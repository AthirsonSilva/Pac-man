FIRST LEVEL - How to play

- Get all the points (pellets) scattered in the field to win.
- Also be careful not to hit any ghost, otherwise you will lose.
- Press the W key to move the character up.
- Press the S key to move the character down.
- Press the A key to move the character left.
- Press the D key to move the character right.
